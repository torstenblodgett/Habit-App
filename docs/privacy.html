Privacy Policy

Scorecard · Last updated: March 2025

Scorecard is a personal daily score and habit tracking app. Your privacy is important. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Data We Collect

None. Scorecard does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, and no third-party tracking.

Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create in Scorecard — your habits, daily scores, and logs — is stored locally on your device using your device's storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed from your device.

Photo Library

Scorecard can generate a shareable day card summarising your daily score. If you choose to save this image, the app will request permission to add it to your photo library. This permission is only used when you explicitly initiate a save action. The app does not read from, browse, or access any existing photos.

Third-Party Services

Scorecard does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or social platforms. No data is sent to external services of any kind.

Children's Privacy

Scorecard does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and contains no age-restricted content.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
